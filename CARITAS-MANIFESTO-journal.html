How to Use This Journal

Welcome to your 40-day journey of learning to love God with everything.
This journal is your companion as you read through the daily devotions in the CARITAS book.

✓ Process what you're learning through reflection questions
✓ Apply truth to your life with specific action steps
✓ Track your growth over 40 days
✓ Pray intentionally using guided prayer prompts
✓ Remember key verses with memory cards
✓ Prepare for group discussions by capturing your thoughts

Tips for Success

📖

Find a Consistent Time

Morning works best for most people—before the day gets busy. But whenever works for you, be consistent.

✍️

Be Honest

This journal is between you and God. Don't write what you think you should say—write what's actually true.

🎯

Be Specific

Vague responses lead to vague transformation. Get specific about struggles, commitments, and applications.

🤝

Share with Your Group

Bring this journal to group meetings. Your reflections will fuel great discussions.

📝

Don't Skip the Hard Questions

The questions that make you uncomfortable are often the ones God wants you to answer.

🙏

Pray Through Your Writing

Let journaling become prayer. Talk to God as you write.
